Open Morning

Open Morning

Date: Sat 10th Nov Time: 10:00-13:00
Location: The Dickinson Centre, Park Community School, Havant, PO9 4BU

Interested in becoming a primary or secondary teacher? This drop-in event offers the perfect opportunity to discuss teacher training options with experts and gain an insight into what the training year entails.

We will provide information on and answer questions regarding the following:

  • Entry requirements for teacher training
  • Applying via UCAS
  • School placements
  • School experience
  • Subject knowledge enhancement (SKE) courses
  • Professional Skills Tests
  • Eligibility criteria for tax-free bursaries
  • QTS & PGCE options
  • The interview process
  • Employment prospects on course completion

This event is suitable for graduates, career changers, Teaching Assistants, Learning Support Assistants and Cover Supervisors.
