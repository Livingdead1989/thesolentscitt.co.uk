Walter Cole - Maths Teacher

"My journey towards teaching started in Australia, was considered in London and finally settled under the tutelage of The Solent SCITT, where their depth and breadth of resources and experience cements them as a key force in teacher training."

My Background

I spent 30 years manufacturing garments and merchandise for the licensees of brands such as: Billabong, Speedo, U2, Rolling Stones, The Eagles, Coldplay and others

Why I wanted to teach

After a wonderfully successful career, I wanted to put something back into the community, specifically helping young people with a key life skill subject - Maths.

Training

I moved from Chelsea to Hampshire to be invigorated by a well-considered mix of students undertaking the course. Importantly, as the course challenged the students there was a depth of experience on offer to assist all the candidates.

Future aspirations

I am currently undertaking an NQT year at the Cowplain School where I hope to further my skill set towards becoming an outstanding teacher.
